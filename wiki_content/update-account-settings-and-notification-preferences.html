Update Your Account Settings

1. Open your account options by clicking on the Account icon in the left-hand corner.

2. Click on option you want to edit

Settings

Under 'Settings', users may add additional contact options like an additional email address or mobile device.  In addition, Canvas users may manage services (Google Drive, Skype, etc) connected with their account and mobile devices connected via the Canvas App.  Lastly, users can modify how they view a course by adding a High Contrast filter or Underline links options.

Setting Communication Channels

Canvas supports notification through email, SMS text message, and Twitter. You account already has your school email address as a communication channel.

If you want to receive notifications as text messages on your phone, click on the Account icon in the left-hand corner and then Settings. Then click the Add Contact Method on the right.

Notifications

Notification Preferences allow users to select how and when they want to be notified when various events occur within a course. Notification settings are applied to all courses. Canvas users can choose how often they want to receive Canvas notifications.

Set Your Notifications

Under Settings, click Notifications on the left. You can hover your mouse over a course activity item to see a description of that notification. Hover over the notification type you want to change and choose to receive a notification immediately, daily, weekly, or never.
