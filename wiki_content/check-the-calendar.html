Click on the Calendar link in the User Dashboard to access a calendar with events and assignments from all your courses.

Use the Calendars list on the right (3) to choose which calendars to display. Click on an assignment in the calendar (1, 4) to submit it. If an assignment has a line through it, that indicates that you have turned it in or the due date has passed.
