Course Layout:

Once the course starts, weekly pages will be available. Each week's overview page will contains the following items and information:

  • Read
    • Contains instructions for which chapters to read in your textbook
  • Watch
    • Contains the lecture material for the chapters covered during the week. There may also be extra videos included
  • Do
    • Case Study assignment
      • This is where you will find a link to the weekly case study assignment
      • Remember, you submit the case studies for the final chapter covered during the week
      • You will also find the previous week's Case Study Solution video
    • Project
      • This is where you will find weekly project assignment
    • Quiz
      • This is where you will take your weekly quiz. Only click on the link when you are read to take the quiz as you only get 1 attempt.
    • Optional Feedback Survey
      • I'm all about improving the course. Use the Optional Feedback Survey link to provide feedback for the week. Anything you provide will only be used to enhance the course and will not be used against you in any way.

Please also review all of the following information in the navigational menu to ensure that you are prepared for the course:

  • Announcements - I will send occasional announcements to remind you of deadlines, inform you of pertinent topics, and so on. You will receive an email each time an announcement is sent. Make sure to read all announcements!
  • Syllabus & Course Schedule - Description of the course and pertinent course related information and a list of topics, assignments, and due dates
  • Modules - The course is divided into Weekly Modules. Each week contains one learning module.
  • Hallway Conversations - Have a question about the course? Ask it in Hallway Conversations.
  • Grades - You can check your grades here, as well as read feedback on assignments.
  • Inbox - to send an email to the instructor.
  • Student Resources - find support available for a variety of student needs
  • ASU Library - Check out books, browse research journals, and more.
  • Help - if you have any difficulties using Canvas please consult with this page to help the appropriate help.
