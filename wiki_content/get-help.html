If you need help using Canvas, you can contact the ASU Help Desk at 855-278-5080.

You can also click on the Help button in the bottom left-hand corner and search the Canvas Guides for a solution.

If you feel Canvas is misbehaving, you can easily report a problem by clicking the Help link and choosing Report a Problem. It is best to do this while on the webpage where the problem is occurring.  

When in doubt, contact Canvas Support via their Hotline or 24/7 Chat Support. They are there to help!

Help with Assignments or Activities

If you need help with an assignment or activity, you can contact the instructor using the Inbox link in the global navigation menu in the left-hand corner. Refer to the syllabus to find the best times and ways to contact your instructor and how quickly you can expect a response.
