Create a form using https://formspree.io:

This feature only works when you host your website in a server, such as github.com.

Steps:

  1. In the html file, head section, add a new meta tag with name="referrer" and content="origin" as the fields. Please see formspreeStep1.JPG.
  2. Use "https://formspree.io/youremail@gmail.com" in the action field, where the youremail@gmail.com is your gmail account.
  3. Inside the form, add a name attribute in every input tag. Please see formspreeStep2.JPG.
  4. Formspree.io will email you a confirmation email.
  5. Open that email and follow the steps in that email to confirm your email.
  6. Then, you will see the message as showed in formspreeConfirmation.JPG.
