Browser

Canvas works best when accessed using Chrome or Firefox. Both browsers are a free download.

 

 

Account Login

You can log in with your login credentials you created upon registration for the course.  If you cannot log in to Canvas and need your password reset, please contact Canvas Help via the link in the lefthand red global navigation bar.

Canvas Apps for Mobile Devices

You can access Canvas on your phone or a tablet using a browser app, such as Safari or Chrome. You can also install the free Canvas app on your mobile devices. Here is a list of current Canvas features available in the mobile apps.

When you start the app for the first time you will need enter our Ursinus Canvas URL, which is https://ursinus.instructure.com
