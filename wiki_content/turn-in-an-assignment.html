You can submit online Assignments in Canvas through several different ways. Instructors can choose what kind of online submissions they wish to allow. They may also give you the option to resubmit assignments. 

To access an Assignment, go to the desired course and click on  the assignment in the appropriate course module.

 

You will then see the assignment.  Click Submit assignment to submit your file:

Click Browse... to locate your file on your computer to select it:

 

After selecting your file, you will see the filename next to the Browse... button.  Click Submit Assignment:

 

Your submission was successful when you see this screen:
