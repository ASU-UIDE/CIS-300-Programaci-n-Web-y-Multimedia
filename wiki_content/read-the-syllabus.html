The Syllabus will list all policies, procedures, assignments, etc. You can access the syllabus by going to the specific course and clicking on the Syllabus link in the menu on the left. 

The Syllabus contains a list of assignments and due dates in a table view called 'Course Summary'.  This will be in every course at the bottom of the Syllabus page.  Click the assignment title to view the details of the assignment.
