Welcome to Canvas Student Orientation! This course will guide you through the nuances of the Canvas learning management system. Take a look at the lessons within Modules. You can go through each module in order or you can jump around.

Note: Each of your Canvas courses may look a little different depending on how your professors have designed them. Be sure to ask your professors where to find something if you are confused.

If you need any assistance in Canvas, here are some helpful resources:

  • Having trouble navigating the Canvas system? Something not working the way it should? Contact the help desk by clicking 'Help' at the bottom left hand side of any Canvas page. You can contact the ASU Help Desk 24/7 via live chat to assist with your Canvas questions or search the Canvas Guides!
