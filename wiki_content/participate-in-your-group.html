Participate in Your Group

There are several different ways to use the Canvas Group Tools to communicate and collaborate with your peers. How to use each tool to complete your work is explained in more detail below. Access the links for step-by-step instructions. You may only use the Group Tools in Canvas to work with your group members so that your instructor can monitor participation.

Accessing your group

Group Tools

Create a Group Page

  • Add instructions, notes, or important information to Group Pages.
  • Only Group Members can view and edit the content of a Page, by default.

View the Group Calendar and Add Events and Reminders 

  • Add important dates to the Group calendar.
  • Only Group members will be able to view these dates.

Send a Group Announcement 

Post Announcements to communicate with your Group members.

  • Announcements are only visible to Group members.
  • New Announcements appear on the Group Home Page/Recent Activity Stream and/or as an email notification (based on your Notification Preferences 

Start a Group Discussion

  • Create Discussions to talk about projects or ask questions.
  • Discussion posts are only visible to Group members.
  • New Discussion posts appear on the Group Home Page/Recent Activity Stream and/or as an email notification (based on your Notification Preferences 

Send a Group Message

  • Send a message to all members of your group and/or your instructor using the Canvas Inbox.
  • Recipients will receive also a copy of the message in their email inbox (based on their Notification Preferences 

Store and Share Group Files

  • Add files to your Group space so that everyone can access them.
  • Files are only shared between and visible to Group members. 

 Participate in a Group Conference

  • Hold a video conference (with up to 50 group members) using Big Blue Button.
  • Only Group members can participate in Conferences.
