Get the Textbook:

For CIS 300, the Basics of Web Design HTML5/CSS by Terry Felke-Morris is the textbook, either in paperback or eBook format works.  

Basics of Web Design: HTML5 & CSS (5th Edition)
By: Terry Felke-Morris
ISBN: 9780135225486

The Basics of Web Design HTML5/CSS textbook has 12 chapters that you work through during the semester. Each chapter discusses one area of website design/development. Typically each chapter contains several hands-on practice problems where the solutions are provided. Additionally, each chapter typically has 1 part of a case study that is continued throughout the textbook. This semester we will be working on the Pacific Trails Resort Case Study.

Download Student Resource Files

Go to https://media.pearsoncmg.com/bc/abp/cs-resources/products/product.html#product,isbn=0135225485, then click on the Companion Website link to download the student files. The companion website provides the image and video files for the homework assignments, solutions for all hands-on practices, and VideoNotes created by the author for all chapters.   Hands-on practices provide great examples in how to do the case study assignments. Make sure you check them out!!
